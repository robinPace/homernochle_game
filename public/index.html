Goal

The aim of the game is to reach 1000 points before the other team, by taking bids, melding with your partner, and taking tricks.

Card Rankings

  • Ace - Highest Ranking, and is worth a point when taking tricks
  • Ten - Second Highest, and is worth a point when taking tricks
  • King - Third Highest, and is worth a point when taking tricks
  • Queen - Fourth Highest, and is not worth a point when taking tricks
  • Jack - Last, and is not worth a point when taking tricks

The Play

Players will take turns bidding starting at 150. Whoever takes the bid will select a trump suit, a suit that will for that round, be worth more. They will then have their partner pass them up to 10 cards in attempt to meld their hands together and get as many points as possible. They must send cards back, so everyone ends with 20 cards. Once cards have been exchanged, players will then go around 'taking tricks'. Initially starting with the bid winner, each player will go around, trying to secure their points or push them to reach their bid amount. Aces, Tens, and Kings are the only cards worth points. This will keep going until all players are out of cards
